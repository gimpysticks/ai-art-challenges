# JuneCandy26 — Simplified Daily Prompt List

**Creator:** @fudonutsunele  
**Challenge Tag:** #JuneCandy26

## June 1–30

1. Cake layers rearrange when you blink. Hazelnuts float in impossible orbits.
2. Rocky road extends into a path that loops back into itself. Marshmallows have tiny faces.
3. A macaroon whispers. Or a candy egg hatches a smaller, stranger egg inside.
4. Cheese ages backward the moment you look away.
5. A gingerbread castle where doors open to different rooms each time.
6. Cake sweats applesauce that forms temporary words.
7. The scoop has a reflection that doesn’t match. It’s a different flavor in the mirror.
8. Rice un-grains into sugar sand. Seaweed ties itself in knots.
9. Filling swirls into double images — faces, landscapes, or someone else’s memory.
10. Ice cream floats above the glass. Root beer bubbles spell a single word.
11. Bread slowly expands and contracts as if breathing.
12. Cookies move independently with shadows. Peanut butter swirls into unknown symbols.
13. Candy changes flavor constantly — but never into a flavor you recognize.
14. Candy flag stripes shift positions when you stare directly at them.
15. Prunes rehydrate into impossible fruit — or into something that was never fruit at all.
16. Fudge crystallizes into stained-glass patterns that change every glance.
17. Root beer bubbles rise sideways. A tiny ship sails in the foam.
18. A peanut butter cookie stretches as you lift it — but never breaks.
19. Red candies pulse like slow heartbeats. Symbols shift into new meanings.
20. A catfish gummy looks one way but tastes another. Its reflection reveals the truth.
21. A sun-shaped candy casts the shadow of a moon. It takes exactly one day to eat.
22. Chocolate éclair cream spells a different word each hour. The word is always almost familiar.
23. Candy refuses to melt — until you stop wanting it to.
24. Pralines crack open to reveal a smaller, older praline inside.
25. Candy tastes like a forgotten afternoon. Or ugly candy tastes like triumph.
26. Candy-inspired salon products cause your reflection to pause before copying you.
27. A rolling candy cart has only two wheels but never falls. Treats float just above the counter.
28. Southern candy where palmetto leaves wave on their own — even indoors.
29. Waffle iron grids ripple like water. Syrup pours upward into the iron.
30. A social media mock-ad reel where each treat remembers the artist who made it.

---

## Prompt Philosophy

- One strong surreal idea per image.
- Keep prompts concise.
- Focus on emotional surrealism rather than worldbuilding.
- Prioritize dream logic over lore.
- Use soft, readable compositions.
- Favor nostalgia, memory, reflection, and impossible food behavior.
- Include at least five audio recommendations with final prompt outputs.
