# JuneCandy26 Creative Direction

This framework intentionally removes:
- Victorian gothic aesthetics
- industrial fantasy systems
- cathedral machinery
- necromantic lore
- overbuilt cinematic mythology
- dense atmospheric stacking

The focus is instead:

- emotional surrealism
- candy dream logic
- nostalgic uncanny imagery
- surreal food behavior
- liminal dessert aesthetics
- soft visual storytelling
- clean dreamcore readability

Prompts should feel:
- emotionally strange
- visually simple
- softly surreal
- dreamlike
- nostalgic
- uncanny but approachable

The surreal mechanic should always be the main focus.

Do not overwhelm the prompt with excessive descriptors.

The strongest images will usually contain:
- one subject
- one impossible behavior
- one emotional atmosphere

Simple visuals with strong surreal concepts are preferred over large cinematic complexity.
