# Core Direction

This chat rebuilt the JuneCandy26 challenge into a simplified candy surrealism prompt system. The old Victorian Gothic / steampunk / cathedral / industrial fantasy / necromantic worldbuilding style was removed.

## Use Instead
- soft surrealism
- dream logic
- emotional uncanny imagery
- nostalgic dreamcore
- candy surrealism
- impossible food behavior
- liminal dessert environments
- soft anime-inspired surrealism
- glossy product-photo realism
- emotional memory distortion

## Avoid
- Victorian aesthetics
- Gothic architecture
- cathedral machinery
- industrial fantasy
- steampunk / dieselpunk
- brass mechanisms
- necromancy
- aristocracy
- lore systems
- recurring worlds / factions
- heavy mythology continuity
- excessive atmospheric stacking

## Prompt Rule Going Forward
Keep MidJourney prompts tight: lead with the concept, use concise visual descriptors, focus on one surreal mechanic, and leave storytelling to the caption.

1. Cake layers rearrange when you blink. Hazelnuts float in impossible orbits.
2. Rocky road extends into a path that loops back into itself. Marshmallows have tiny faces.
3. A macaroon whispers. Or a candy egg hatches a smaller, stranger egg inside.
4. Cheese ages backward the moment you look away.

13. Candy changes flavor constantly — but never into a flavor you recognize.

15. Prunes rehydrate into impossible fruit — or into something that was never fruit at all.
16. Fudge crystallizes into stained-glass patterns that change every glance.
17. Root beer bubbles rise sideways. A tiny ship sails in the foam.
18. Peanut butter cookie stretches as you lift it — but never breaks.
19. Red candies pulse like heartbeats. Symbols shift into new meanings.
20. Catfish gummy tastes different than it looks. Reflection reveals truth.
21. Sun-shaped candy casts a moon shadow. It takes exactly one day to eat.
22. Chocolate éclair cream spells a different word every hour.
23. Candy refuses to melt until you stop wanting it to.
24. Praline reveals an older praline inside.

27. Candy cart rolls on two wheels and never falls.
