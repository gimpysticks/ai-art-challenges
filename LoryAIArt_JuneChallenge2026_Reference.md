# @lory.ai.art — #junechallenge

## Challenge Theme

This challenge is based on:

- Proverbs
- Folk wisdom
- Aphorisms
- Life lessons
- Human behavior
- Symbolic storytelling
- Visual metaphors

The goal is not to illustrate the proverb literally.

The goal is to create a cinematic visual interpretation of the wisdom contained within the proverb.

Each proverb becomes:

- A unique world
- A unique story
- A unique visual metaphor
- A unique narrative moment

No recurring worlds are preloaded.

Worlds are created organically from the meaning of each proverb.

## Prompt Construction Rules

- The daily proverb must appear at the beginning of the prompt.
- Build a unique world from the proverb.
- Avoid recurring worlds unless intentionally established.
- Favor symbolism over literal interpretation.
- Favor cinematic storytelling.
- Favor strong visual metaphors.
- Create a concise Instagram-friendly caption.
- Provide 3–5 audio recommendations.
- Use a maximum of 5 hashtags.
- Include:
  - @lory.ai.art
  - #junechallenge
  - #gimpysticks

## Core Question

For each day ask:

"What visual metaphor best represents this proverb?"
